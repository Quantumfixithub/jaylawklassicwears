Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our services and policies.

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by calling us at +234 803 456 7890, sending us an email, or filling out the contact form above. We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance to ensure availability.

What should I bring for my first appointment?

For tailoring consultations, bring any reference images, fabric samples if you have them, and your measurements if available. For training inquiries, just bring your questions and enthusiasm to learn!

How long does custom tailoring take?

Standard custom pieces typically take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity and our current workload. Rush orders can be accommodated for an additional fee. We'll provide a specific timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans for both tailoring services and training programs. Typically, we require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon completion. Contact us to discuss options that work for your budget.

What is your cancellation policy?

Appointments can be rescheduled or cancelled up to 24 hours in advance without penalty. For custom orders in progress, cancellations may incur fees depending on the stage of completion. We'll always work with you to find the best solution.

Do you provide delivery services?

Yes! We offer delivery within Abuja and can arrange shipping to other locations in Nigeria. Delivery fees vary based on location. International shipping is also available for special orders.

Can I visit without an appointment?

Walk-ins are welcome during business hours! However, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure we can give you our full attention and avoid wait times, especially on weekends.

Do you work with corporate clients?

Absolutely! We have extensive experience with corporate uniforms, branded apparel, and bulk orders. We offer special pricing for corporate clients and can accommodate large-scale projects. Contact us to discuss your corporate needs.
