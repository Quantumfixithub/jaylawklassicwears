Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our training programs.

Do I need prior experience to enroll?

No prior experience is required for our beginner courses. We welcome complete beginners and provide all the foundational knowledge you need. For intermediate and advanced courses, some basic sewing knowledge is recommended.

What materials do I need to bring?

All major equipment including sewing machines, cutting tables, and tools are provided. You only need to bring fabric for your projects (we'll provide a list), a notebook, and your enthusiasm to learn!

Are payment plans available?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans for all courses. You can pay in 2-3 installments depending on the program. Contact us to discuss the best payment option for you.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Absolutely! All students who successfully complete their courses receive a professional certificate recognized in the fashion industry. We also provide portfolio guidance and reference letters.

What is the class size?

We maintain small class sizes (10-20 students maximum) to ensure personalized attention and hands-on guidance from instructors. This allows for better learning outcomes and individual support.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

Yes! We have partnerships with fashion houses, boutiques, and design studios. We also provide business mentorship for those wanting to start their own fashion businesses, including guidance on branding, marketing, and client acquisition.
